# Key Bindings

|Key|Action|
|Right arrow/Enter|[On dir] Go to dir. [On file] Open file.|
|-----------|----------------------------------------|
|Left arrow|Go back. (Equivalent of `cd ..`)|
|----------|--------------------------------|
|Up arrow| Scroll Up |
|--------|-----------|
|Down arrow| Scroll down |
|----------|-------------|
|`Ctrl-q`; `Shift-q`; `Ctrl-c`|Quit the application|
|-----------------------------|--------------------|
|Just type| Fuzzy Finding(fzf)|
|---------|-------------------|
|Space|Highlight|
|-----|---------|
